CozyScene Privacy Policy

Overview

This app is designed to provide a simple, immersive ambient viewing experience on Apple TV. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No personal data is collected
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used
  • No advertising or third-party SDKs are integrated

Data Usage

Because no personal data is collected, no data is used, processed, or shared for any purpose.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party services that collect user data.
